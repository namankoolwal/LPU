LOVELY PROFESSIONAL UNIVERSITY

Lovely Professional University (LPU) is a Private University situated on the outskirts of Jalandhar, Punjab. LPU is the largest single-campus university in India, with more than 30,000 students offering 300+ programs across its 20 Schools. LPU is ranked at 47th position in NIRF Ranking 2022 among all Private and Government universities in India. LPU has been ranked 23rd among the Top Universities of India as per the Times Higher Education World University Rankings 2023. LPU is also globally acknowledged with its world ranking being in the 801-1000 Band as per Times Higher Education placing it in the same band as BITS Pilani, IIT Patna, and IIT Gandhinagar.
